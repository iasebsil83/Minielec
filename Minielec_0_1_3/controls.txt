Move forward  : Z or z
     backward : Q or q
     right    : D or d
     left     : Q or q
     up       : [space]
     down     : W or w

Select toolbar element 1 : 1 or &
					   2 : 2 or é
					   3 : 3 or "
					   4 : 4 or '
					   5 : 5 or (
					   6 : 6 or -
					   7 : 7 or è

Come back to main menu : M or m

Switch left/right toolbar : 0 or à

Toggle sound in game : < or >

Take screenshot : + or =

Save scene : t

Load saved scene : r

IMMUTABLE KEYS

Toggle mouse view/use mode : right click
Use mouse tool : left click
